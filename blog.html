My favorite GTD app: Wunderlist

10/04/15

From your daily todo list to organzing your ideas for your next site Wunderlist does it all. The interface is clean and it also has fun kitten backgrounds for your workspace. Lists can also be shared with other Wunderlist users for easy collaboration on projects. I recently used this with a friend to jot down ideas we thought of of a site I am helping build.
